Copyright and Legal

Is it legal to use Barbie-style fonts?

Yes, using fonts inspired by the Barbie aesthetic is generally legal for personal use. However, the official Barbie logo and trademark are owned by Mattel. Our fonts are similar styles rather than exact replicas, making them safe for most uses.

Do I need to give credit when using these fonts?

Troubleshooting

The text isn't copying properly. What should I do?

Try these solutions:

  1. Click the copy button again
  2. Manually select and copy the text
  3. Try a different browser
  4. Check if your browser has clipboard permissions enabled

The fonts aren't displaying correctly on my device

This might be due to browser compatibility issues. Try:

  • Updating your browser to the latest version
  • Clearing your browser cache
  • Trying a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Checking your internet connection
